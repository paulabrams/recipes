# 🥬 Grilled Savoy Cabbage

## Ingredients
- 1 head savoy cabbage
- 3 tablespoons olive oil
- Salt and freshly ground black pepper
- ½ teaspoon garlic powder
- 1 lemon

### Optional Finish
- Melted butter or extra olive oil
- Shaved parmesan
- Red pepper flakes

⸻

## Instructions

1. **Prepare the cabbage**
   Cut the head of savoy cabbage into 4–6 wedges, keeping the core intact so the leaves hold together. Drizzle generously with olive oil and season with salt, pepper, and garlic powder.

2. **Preheat the grill**
   Set the pellet grill to **350°F** and let it come up to temperature.

3. **Grill the wedges**
   Place the cabbage wedges cut-side down directly on the grates. Grill for **8–10 minutes per side**, flipping once, until you get nice char marks and the outer leaves are crispy but the interior is still tender with a slight bite. Total cook time is roughly 20–25 minutes.

4. **Finish and serve**
   Remove from grill and squeeze fresh lemon juice over the wedges. Drizzle with melted butter or olive oil. Add shaved parmesan or red pepper flakes if desired.

⸻

## ✅ Yield & Serving
- Serves 4–6 as a side dish
- Pairs well with grilled pork chops cooked at the same temperature

⸻

## 💡 Tips & Variations
- Cook alongside pork chops at 350°F — they'll finish around the same time
- Pull pork chops at 145°F internal temperature
- A squeeze of lemon before grilling adds brightness to the char
- Keep the core intact or the wedges will fall apart on the grates
