# Tuscan White Bean Soup

## Ingredients
- [ ] 1 pound mild Italian sausage
- [ ] 2 tablespoons butter
- [ ] 1/2 tablespoon olive oil
- [ ] 1 large onion, diced
- [ ] 2 large carrots, sliced or diced
- [ ] 3 ribs celery, sliced
- [ ] 2-3 garlic cloves, minced
- [ ] 2 tablespoons tomato paste
- [ ] 1 teaspoon Italian seasoning
- [ ] 2 tablespoons fresh basil, chopped
- [ ] 1 teaspoon ground black pepper
- [ ] 1/2 teaspoon crushed red pepper
- [ ] 1 teaspoon kosher salt
- [ ] 6 cups chicken broth (1 32oz box + 1 15oz can)
- [ ] 2 cans Great Northern Beans, drained
- [ ] 1 cup goat yogurt
- [ ] 3 cups fresh baby spinach, rough chopped
- [ ] 1 tablespoon fresh lemon juice
- [ ] Parmesan or Romano cheese for topping

## Directions
1. Peel and slice carrots, dice celery, dice onion, and mince garlic.
2. In large stock pot over medium heat, add butter and olive oil. Add Italian sausage and cook until browned.
3. Add onion, carrots, and celery. Cook until carrots begin to soften, stirring occasionally.
4. Add tomato paste, garlic, Italian seasoning, black pepper, crushed red pepper, and kosher salt. Stir and cook for 60 seconds.
5. Add chicken broth and bring to a simmer.
6. Add drained Great Northern Beans and fresh basil. Stir.
7. Remove from heat. Stir in goat yogurt (off heat to prevent curdling).
8. Return to low heat. Rough chop spinach, add and stir until wilted.
9. Squeeze in 1 tablespoon of fresh lemon juice. Stir.
10. Top with Pecorino Romano or goat cheese.

## Tips
- Delicious with crusty bread or garlic bread

## Notes
- Great with rye bread for Wilbur trip
