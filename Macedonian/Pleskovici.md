# Плескавици (Pleskovici - Balkan Meat Patties)

## Ingredients

- 1 kg мелено месо (1 kg ground meat)
- 2-3 главици кромид (2-3 onion bulbs)
- 2-3 јајца (2-3 eggs)
- 1 парче сол и 1 лажица вагета (1 piece salt and 1 tablespoon vegeta seasoning)
- 2-3 лажици сувени презли (2-3 tablespoons dried breadcrumbs)

## Instructions

Mix everything well together and form into patties.
