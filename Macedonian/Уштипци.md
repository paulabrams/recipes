# Уштипци од Бело Месо (White Meat Fritters)

## Ingredients

- 2 бели меса (2 chicken breasts)
- 150 g кашкавал, тенчан (150 g kashkaval cheese, thinly sliced)
- 100 g сланина, сецкана (100 g bacon, chopped)
- 1 парче леб без кора, потопено во млеко (1 slice bread without crust, soaked in milk)
- 1 јајце (1 egg)
- Сол, вагета, црн пипер, бел лук (salt, vegeta seasoning, black pepper, garlic)

## Instructions

The white meat is ground. All the other ingredients are added and the mixture is mixed well. Form into fritters (уштипци), roll in flour, egg, and breadcrumbs, then fry in hot oil.
