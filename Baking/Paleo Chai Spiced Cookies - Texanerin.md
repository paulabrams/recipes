# Paleo Chai Spiced Cookies (grain-free, gluten-free, dairy-free)

## Ingredients
- [ ] 2 cups (200 grams) blanched almond flour
- [ ] 1/4 cup (34 grams) coconut flour (sifted if lumpy)
- [ ] 1/4 cup (50 grams) coconut sugar
- [ ] 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
- [ ] 1/4 teaspoon cream of tartar
- [ ] 3/4 teaspoon ground ginger
- [ ] 3/4 teaspoon ground cinnamon
- [ ] 1/4 teaspoon ground cardamom
- [ ] 1/4 teaspoon ground cloves
- [ ] 1/4 teaspoon salt
- [ ] 1/4 cup (56 grams) coconut oil, melted
- [ ] 1/4 cup (60 ml) maple syrup
- [ ] 1 teaspoon vanilla
- [ ] 1 large egg (50 grams out of shell, room temp)
- [ ] 3-4 tablespoons sugar for rolling (raw sugar or coconut sugar for paleo)

## Directions

### 1. Prepare Dough
1. In a medium mixing bowl, stir together the dry ingredients (almond flour through salt)
2. In a large mixing bowl, mix together the melted coconut oil, maple syrup, vanilla and egg
3. Add the dry mixture to the wet and stir just until combined

### 2. Chill and Shape
1. Wrap the dough in plastic wrap and refrigerate for 2 hours or until firm enough to roll into balls
2. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper

### 3. Bake
1. Roll the dough into 1" balls
2. Roll in sugar and place 2" apart on the prepared baking sheet
3. Bake for 8-10 minutes or until the tops are firm and no longer wet
4. Remove to a baking sheet to cool completely

## Tips
- Cookies will not brown but may crack a little when done
- For a richer flavor, use almond extract instead of vanilla
- Can substitute garbanzo flour for coconut flour if desired
- Can be made vegan using a flax egg

## Notes
- Makes about 22 cookies
- Total time: 28 minutes (20 min prep, 8 min cook)
- Store in an airtight container for up to 5 days
- Can be frozen
- Adapted from A Clean Bake's Grain-free Thick and Chewy Snickerdoodles
