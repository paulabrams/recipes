# Wild Blueberry Crumble (Vegan, Gluten-Free)

## Ingredients

### For the Filling
- [ ] 4 cups frozen wild blueberries
- [ ] 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
- [ ] 1 tablespoon arrowroot or cornstarch

### For the Crumble Topping
- [ ] 1 cup old-fashioned rolled oats (gluten-free if needed)
- [ ] 1/2 cup pecans, chopped
- [ ] 1/4 cup almond flour
- [ ] 3 tablespoons maple syrup
- [ ] 3 tablespoons coconut oil, melted
- [ ] 1 teaspoon cinnamon
- [ ] 1/4 teaspoon sea salt

## Directions

### 1. Prepare Blueberries
1. Preheat oven to 375Â°F
2. Grease an 8x8 inch baking dish
3. Add frozen blueberries to the dish
4. Drizzle with vanilla extract
5. Sprinkle arrowroot or cornstarch over blueberries and toss to coat

### 2. Make Crumble Topping
1. In a medium bowl, mix together rolled oats, chopped pecans, almond flour, cinnamon, and sea salt
2. Add melted coconut oil and maple syrup
3. Stir until well combined and mixture is crumbly

### 3. Assemble and Bake
1. Sprinkle the crumble topping evenly over the blueberries
2. Gently press down slightly to pack it down
3. Bake for 30-35 minutes until topping is golden brown and blueberries are bubbly
4. Let cool for at least 10 minutes before serving

## Tips
- Use frozen wild blueberries for best flavor and intense berry taste
- Can use fresh blueberries if in season
- Serve warm with a scoop of ice cream or coconut whipped cream
- Make sure coconut oil is melted for easier mixing
- Adjust sweetness by adding more maple syrup if desired

## Notes
- Serves 6-8 people
- Total time: 45 minutes
- Naturally sweetened with maple syrup
- Gluten-free and vegan
- Freezes well for up to 3 months
