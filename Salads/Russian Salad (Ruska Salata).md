# Russian Salad (Ruska Salata)

## Ingredients
- [ ] 300 g potatoes
- [ ] 300 g carrots
- [ ] 300 g peas
- [ ] 300 g pickles
- [ ] 300 g ham
- [ ] 300 g cheese
- [ ] 300 g mayonnaise

## Directions
1. Boil all vegetables (not the pickles), making sure they are not overcooked to retain their shape, then finely chop into small cubes.
2. Chop ham and cheese and mix in a deep bowl.
3. Chop pickles.
4. Add the mayonnaise at the end and mix well. If you want to add something else, also stir into the mixture. Some add boiled eggs to the salad.
5. Serve in deeper bowls or as desired. Coat with a thin layer of mayonnaise and decorate as desired. Keep refrigerated until serving.

## Notes
- This salad is often served for bigger occasions and holidays, especially New Year's Eve
- Traditionally paired with warm homemade kifli
- You can adjust the recipe and increase or decrease the amount of ingredients
- Source: [Macedonian Cuisine](https://www.macedoniancuisine.com/2016/01/russian-salad-ruska-salata.html?m=1)
